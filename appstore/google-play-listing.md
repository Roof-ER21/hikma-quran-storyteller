## Full Description
Alaya & Soad's Gift helps families learn the Quran with safe kids mode, trusted stories, and recitation tools designed for everyday use.

Why families choose Alaya & Soad's Gift:
- Child-safe by default with parent controls.
- Reliable offline access for Quran and stories.
- Clear subscription and restore flow.
- Progress backup/export tools to prevent data loss.

What you can do in the app:

1. Prophet Stories
Read and listen to 24 prophet stories from the Quran with meaningful lessons and clear narration.

2. Kids Mode
Give children a guided, age-appropriate learning space with parent gate and progress tracking.

3. Quran Reader and Recitation
Read Quran text with translation support and listen with 20+ reciters.

4. AI Tutor
Ask questions about Quran stories and learning topics. Parents can turn AI tutor on or off at any time.

5. Islamic Tools
Use prayer times, Qibla direction, and calendar features in one place.

6. Offline + Progress Safety
Download key content for offline learning. Use local backup/restore and optional cloud sync for safer progress continuity.
