Privacy Policy

Alaya & Soad's Gift: Stories from Jannah

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

Our Commitment: Your privacy and your child's safety are our top priorities. This app is designed to provide Islamic education and Quran learning in a safe, privacy-focused environment. We collect minimal data and do not share your information with third parties except as explicitly described below.

1. Developer Information

Developer: Roof-ER21
App Name: Alaya & Soad's Gift: Stories from Jannah
Bundle ID: com.roofer21.alayasoad
Target Audience: Families, children aged 4+, and adults

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Parent Name: Used for account creation and personalization
  • Parent PIN: Stored securely (hashed with bcrypt) for account authentication
  • Child's Name (Optional): For personalizing the learning experience
  • Learning Progress: Stars earned, levels completed, badges, reading history, and practice sessions

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Location Data (When In Use): Used only for calculating accurate prayer times and Qibla direction. Location is never stored or shared.
  • Microphone Access: Used for Quran recitation practice and pronunciation feedback. Audio is processed in real-time and never stored on our servers.
  • Speech Recognition: Used for voice search and recitation checking. Voice data is processed via Google's Gemini AI API and not retained.

2.3 Local Storage (On Your Device)

  • IndexedDB/Local Storage: Stores bookmarks, reading progress, completed activities, and offline content for app functionality
  • Authentication Token: Secure JWT token for maintaining your login session

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Educational Experience: Track learning progress, provide personalized recommendations, and award achievements
  • Prayer Times & Qibla: Calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction based on your location
  • AI Features: Generate Islamic stories, provide pronunciation feedback, and offer interactive tutoring
  • Account Management: Authenticate users, sync progress across devices, and maintain secure access
  • Offline Functionality: Enable app features when not connected to the internet

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Gemini AI API

We use Google's Gemini AI API for:

  • Generating Islamic stories and educational content
  • Text-to-speech for story narration
  • AI tutoring and interactive learning features
  • Speech recognition for pronunciation feedback

Data Shared: Text prompts, voice input for speech recognition (processed in real-time, not stored)
Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 AlQuran.cloud API

We use AlQuran.cloud for retrieving Quran text and translations.

  • Authentication: None required
  • Data Shared: API requests only (no personal information)
  • Privacy Policy: https://alquran.cloud/privacy

4.3 Islamic Network CDN

We stream Quran audio recitations from Islamic Network's CDN.

  • Data Shared: None (public CDN access)
  • Privacy Policy: https://islamic.network/privacy

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Password Security: PINs are hashed using bcrypt before storage
  • Secure Transmission: All API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Database Security: PostgreSQL with SSL connections on Railway infrastructure
  • API Key Protection: API keys are stored server-side only, never exposed to the client
  • Session Management: JWT tokens with 7-day expiration for secure authentication

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Important: This app is designed for families with children aged 4 and above. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

  • Parental Control: All accounts require parent authentication via PIN
  • Minimal Data Collection: We only collect optional child names and learning progress
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Children's data is never shared with third parties for marketing or advertising
  • No Advertising: This app contains no advertisements or tracking pixels
  • No Social Features: No chat, messaging, or social networking features that could expose children to external contacts
  • Parental Rights: Parents can request deletion of all child data at any time (see Section 8)

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we have stored about you
  • Correction: Update or correct any inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Opt-Out: Disable microphone or location permissions in your device settings at any time
  • Data Portability: Request an export of your learning progress data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in Section 11.

9. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained as long as your account remains active
  • Account Deletion: All data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
  • Inactive Accounts: Accounts inactive for 2+ years may be automatically deleted with prior notice

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored on servers located in different countries:

  • Hosting: Railway (United States infrastructure)
  • AI Processing: Google Cloud (may process data across multiple regions)

We ensure that all data transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and use appropriate safeguards.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Displaying an in-app notification on your next login
  • Requiring acceptance of the updated policy for continued use (for material changes)

12. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your data based on:

  • Consent: For optional features like microphone and location access
  • Contractual Necessity: To provide the educational services you request
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve app functionality and maintain security
